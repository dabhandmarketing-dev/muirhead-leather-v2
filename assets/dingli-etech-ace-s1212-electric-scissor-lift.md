**Key Features:**

*   ‍**Electric Power & Efficiency:** The **ETECH ACE S1212** is powered by an **advanced AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) battery system**, delivering **clean, efficient, and reliable performance** for both **indoor and outdoor** slab applications.**‍**
*   **Compact & Versatile Design:** Engineered for **precision and manoeuvrability**, this scissor lift supports a **maximum capacity of 320kg**, making it ideal for **various work environments** without sacrificing stability or performance.**‍**
*   **Precision Elevation:** Designed for **tasks requiring accuracy at height**, the ETECH ACE S1212 provides a **working height of 14 metres (47 feet)** and a **maximum platform height of 12 metres (40 feet)**, ensuring safe and controlled elevation for high-reach tasks.

‍

**Advanced Engineering & Durability**  

Built with **Dingli’s industry-leading engineering**, the **ETECH ACE S1212** is designed for **long-lasting durability and precision**, making it a **reliable solution for slab applications**. Its robust construction ensures **smooth operation, enhanced safety, and superior performance** across various job sites.

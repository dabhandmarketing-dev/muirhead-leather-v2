Designed for **outdoor construction and industrial applications**, the **LGMG AR Series** offers a **rugged, reliable, and easy-to-operate solution** for working at height. Its **simple yet robust design**, combined with advanced industrial features, ensures **efficiency, safety, and ease of use**. The **standard jib** extends reach, making it ideal for accessing **hard-to-reach areas**.

‍

**Key Features:**

*   ‍**Superior Off-Road Performance:** **Oscillating axles** and **four-wheel drive** provide **enhanced stability and superior gradeability**, allowing smooth operation on rough and uneven terrain.**‍**
*   **Precision & Control:** **Proportional controls** ensure **smooth driving, accurate positioning, and a comfortable operating experience**.**‍**
*   **Maximised Productivity:** A **self-levelling platform** combined with **hydraulic platform rotation** allows for **seamless adjustments**, improving efficiency on the job site.**‍**
*   **Easy Maintenance & Servicing:** The **swing-out engine tray** allows for **quick access**, simplifying maintenance and reducing downtime.**‍**
*   **Smart Diagnostics:** An **intelligent self-diagnostic system** enables **fast troubleshooting**, ensuring minimal disruptions and optimal machine performance.

This **heavy-duty articulating boom lift** is built to withstand demanding job site conditions, making it a **reliable choice for construction and industrial applications**.

The LGMG H625E, a pioneering electric telehandler, boasts a 2500kg capacity and a maximum lift height of 5.94m.

It features a swappable 80V Lithium-Ion battery and a PMSM lifting motor for precise hydraulic control.

Offering three steering modes, it adapts to tight workspaces with ease.

The H625E ensures operator safety with a FOPS&ROPS cab and supports diverse attachments for tasks like forklifting, lifting, loading, grabbing, clamping, and manned operations, making it a highly versatile, multi-purpose machine.

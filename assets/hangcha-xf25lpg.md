### **Optimised Stability & Performance**  

Designed with a **low centre of gravity**, the XF2 forklift delivers **exceptional stability** while maintaining **high residual capacities of up to 3.5T**. The newly engineered **steering axle** enhances control, ensuring smoother and safer operation in demanding environments. Powered by an **award-winning, fuel-efficient EU Stage V engine**, tested for over **10,000 hours**, this forklift guarantees **long-term reliability and cost-effective performance**.

### **Superior Visibility & Operator Comfort**  

A **redesigned mast** provides **outstanding forward visibility**, allowing for **greater precision and control**. The **angled counterweight** at the rear improves **rear-view visibility**, especially when reversing. To enhance comfort, the XF2 is equipped with a **fully suspended gearbox, seat, and overhead guard**, significantly reducing **vibration and operator fatigue** during extended use.

### **Advanced Wet Disc Braking System**

The XF2 Series comes **standard with a wet disc brake transmission**, delivering **superior braking performance**, even in **wet or dusty environments**. This advanced system enhances **brake reliability** while reducing wear and maintenance costs. Additionally, **waterproof electrical connectors** provide extra **protection and durability**, ensuring reliable operation in all conditions.
